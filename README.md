LSM LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Teacher
Register Account – The teacher can create a personal account in the system by providing necessary information such as name, email, and password, which allows them to securely access all the features of the Learning Management System.
Login Account – After registration, the teacher can log into the system using their credentials so they can manage their Grades, classes, assignments, and student records anytime.
Access the Dashboard – Once logged in, the teacher is directed to a dashboard where they can easily view and manage all important information such as announcements, assignments, grades, and student activities in one place.
Teacher can put the assignment – The teacher can create and upload assignments for students by adding instructions, deadlines, and any necessary files, making it easy for students to understand and complete their tasks.
Teacher can announce – The teacher can post announcements to inform students about important updates, schedules, reminders, or any class-related information that needs to be communicated quickly.
Teacher can put grades – The teacher can input and manage students’ grades for assignments, quizzes, and exams, ensuring that all academic performance records are properly stored and updated.
Teachers can print the grades of the students – The teacher has the ability to generate and print grade reports for students, which can be used for documentation, evaluation, or sharing with parents and school administrators.
Teacher can record their attendance – The teacher can mark and track student attendance for each class session, helping maintain accurate records of student participation and presence.
Teacher can access the students – The teacher can view and manage student profiles, allowing them to monitor student progress, performance, and participation in the course.
Teacher can access the submission of the assignment – The teacher can review, download, and evaluate student submissions, making it easier to check completed work and provide feedback.


Students
Register Account – Students can create their own accounts by entering required details, which allows them to securely access their classes and course materials within the system.
Login Account – After registering, students can log into the system using their username and password to access their dashboard and academic information.
Access the Dashboard – Once logged in, students can view a dashboard that displays announcements, assignments, grades, and other important updates related to their classes.
Student sees the announcement of the teacher – Students can read announcements posted by the teacher, ensuring they stay updated with important class information and instructions.
Student sees the assignment that is given – Students can view all assigned tasks, including instructions and deadlines, so they can complete their work on time.
Students see their grades and they can print their grades – Students can check their academic performance through the system and also print their grades for personal records or submission requirements.
Students can submit their work – Students can upload and submit their assignments directly through the system, making it easier to turn in work without needing physical copies.
